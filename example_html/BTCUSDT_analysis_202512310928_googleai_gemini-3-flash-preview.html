Risk Considerations & Educational Context

Educational Context: The "Squeeze"

In technical analysis, a "Squeeze" occurs when volatility (measured by Bollinger Bands) falls to historically low levels. Traders often use this as a leading indicator for a volatility breakout. However, the squeeze does not predict direction, only that a move is coming. Traders often look at the MACD or Directional Index (DI) to guess the breakout direction.

Historical Context Takeaway

The Death Cross (50 SMA below 200 SMA) is historically a bearish signal, but in "choppy" years, it can produce "whipsaws" (false signals). Given that BTC is still 55% above its 200-week SMA, the current daily bearishness might be a healthy correction in a long-term bull cycle.
